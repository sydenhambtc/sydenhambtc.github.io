SBTC Badges

I Hiked the Sydenham Section

The "I Hiked the Sydenham Section" badge is shown on the right and costs $5.00. You may earn it by walking the entire length of the main portion (white blazes) of the Sydenham section of the Bruce Trail. As you walk the section keep a log of what you did and when. Once you have completed the trail send your log, cheque (payable to Sydenham Bruce Trail Club - no cash please), your name, your BTC membership number, your email address, and a stamped, self-addressed envelope:

Sydenham Bruce Trail Club
Box 431
Owen Sound, ON
N4K 5P7

Sydenham End-to-End

This badge is for individuals who register for and complete a Sydenham End-to-End organized by the Sydenham Club.

To commemorate Sydenham BTC’s 50th Anniversary, in 2012 the SBTC re-formatted the End-to-End as an 8-hike series, spread over four weekends, two days each, in the spring and autumn.

"Looping Through Sydenham 25" Challenge

This special badge can be earned by hiking all the loop hikes in the “Looping Through Sydenham” book. (Unfortunately the Laycock Caves loop, hike #8, was shut down in 2017, so only 24 hikes remain available at this time.) To begin your challenge, simply copy the Table of Contents to use as your hiking log. Complete each hike in any order you choose, and note the date on your log. On maps showing more than one loop, just pick your favourite.

The challenge runs from 2014 to 2019; only hikes after March 15, 2014 count. When you complete all 24 hikes, send your log, and a cheque for $5 (payable to Sydenham Bruce Trail Club - no cash please), your name, your BTC membership number, your email address, and a stamped, self-addressed envelope:

Sydenham Bruce Trail Club
Box 431
Owen Sound, ON
N4K 5P7
