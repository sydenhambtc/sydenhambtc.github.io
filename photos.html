2018 Annual General Meeting

Sunday, September 16, 2018

As part of our 2018 AGM, members hiked to our new conservation land at Kemble Wetland Nature Reserve. Thanks to generous donations, the Bruce Trail Conservancy has added another 100 acres onto the adjacent Kemble Rock Nature Reserve. Our Trail Director, Ron Savage, with Sydenham volunteers are working hard to create a new section of trail. The hike was followed by Board members presenting their annual reports and a delicious potluck dinner.

2017 Annual General Meeting

Sunday, September 17th, 2017

As part of our 2017 AGM, members hiked a brand new trail at Kemble Rock Nature Reserve! Thanks to generous donations, the Bruce Trail Conservancy has preserved 200 acres of conservation land in the Kemble Rock area. Our Trail Director, Ron Savage, with Sydenham volunteers have worked hard to create this new trail. The hike was followed by Board members presenting their annual reports and a delicious potluck dinner.

Bruce Trail Day 2015

Bruce Trail Day 2015 was celebrated in Sydenham Club on October 4 at the Pottawatomi Conservation Area near Springmount. Approximately 30 people enjoyed guided hikes around the Merle Gunby Side Trail loop.
Hike Leader Ron Savage explains the geography above Jones Falls.

SBTC End to End 2015

Thanks to all the volunteers who helped make the 2015 SBTC E2E another very successful event. Everyone - the Parking Attendants, Checkpoint / Endpoint Staffers, Bakers, Sweeps, Rovers - did a terrific job which is greatly appreciated. There were 126 registered hikers of which 74 earned the "Blantyre to Wiarton - Sydenham End-to-End" badge. This is one of our clubs biggest fundraisers. It could not have been done without your help.  The photo below says it all. Thanks a lot.
